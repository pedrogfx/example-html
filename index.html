About DayZ

DayZ is a survival video game developed and published by Bohemia Interactive. It is set in an open-world post-apocalyptic environment where players must scavenge for resources, food, water, and weapons to survive.

Gameplay

In DayZ, players can explore the vast landscape, interact with other survivors, build shelters, hunt for food, and engage in both PvE and PvP encounters. The game is known for its challenging survival mechanics and intense player interactions.
